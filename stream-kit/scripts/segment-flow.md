# Segment flow (MVP)

1) Warmup (10 min)
- Hello, quick recap of the day
- Shoutout new viewers

2) React (30 min)
- 3 clips max
- Ask chat for takes

3) IRL stories (20 min)
- 1 story from BXL/PAR/NYC
- 1 life update

4) Chill Q&A (15 min)
- 5 questions from chat

5) Outro (5 min)
- Recap + next stream
